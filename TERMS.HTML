TERMS AND CONDITIONS

We are committed to providing our students with an excellent education in a safe and nurturing environment.
Please take a moment to read our terms and conditions carefully before enrolling your child at our school.
Admissions Policy
Admission to MarkJunior School is subject to availability and our admission criteria. Our admission decisions are based on academic and personal criteria, and we reserve the right to reject any application that does not meet our standards.
Fees and Payments
Tuition and fees at MarkJunior School are subject to change each academic year. The tuition fee covers tuition, textbooks, and access to school facilities. All fees are non-refundable, and any outstanding balances must be paid in full before the start of the academic year.
Code of Conduct
All students at MarkJunior School are expected to follow our code of conduct, which promotes respect, responsibility, and safety. Any violation of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action, including suspension or expulsion.
Health and Safety
The health and safety of our students are of utmost importance. We have policies and procedures in place to ensure the safety of our students, staff, and visitors. All students must comply with our health and safety guidelines, including vaccinations and regular health check-ups.
Privacy Policy
MarkJunior School respects your privacy and will only collect and use your personal information in accordance with our privacy policy. We will not share your personal information with any third party without your consent.

You may not use, copy, reproduce, or distribute any content from our website without our written permission.
Termination
We reserve the right to terminate your child's enrollment at MarkJunior School for any reason, including but not limited to academic, disciplinary, or financial reasons. By enrolling your child at MarkJunior School, you agree to comply with our terms and conditions. We reserve the right to amend these terms and conditions at any time without notice. Please check our website regularly for updates.
