ADMISSION PROCESS

Thank you for considering MarkJunior School for your child's education. We are excited to guide you through our admission process and welcome you to our community. Our admission process comprises several steps to ensure that we admit students who meet our academic and personal criteria and will thrive in our school environment. Step 1: Online Application The first step of the admission process is to complete our online application form. The application form is available on our school website, and it requires basic personal and academic information, as well as supporting documents such as transcripts, recommendation letters, and a recent passport-sized photograph. You can submit the application along with the supporting documents online. Step 2: Application Review After receiving your application, our admission committee reviews it to ensure that you meet our admission criteria. The committee evaluates your academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and potential to contribute positively to our school community. Step 3: Interview If you meet our admission criteria, we will invite you for an interview with our faculty members. The interview is an opportunity for us to learn more about you, your personality, interests, and goals. It also provides you with an opportunity to ask questions and learn more about our school's philosophy, programs, and culture. Step 4: Admission Decision After reviewing your application and conducting the interview, our admission committee evaluates your application and makes an admission decision. We will notify you of our decision via email or phone. Step 5: Enrollment If you are offered admission, you will be required to complete the enrollment process by submitting the necessary documents and paying the required fees. You will receive detailed instructions on the enrollment process along with the admission offer letter. We understand that choosing the right school for your child is a crucial decision, and we encourage you to visit our campus, attend our information sessions, and interact with our faculty and staff to learn more about our school's programs and culture. Please feel free to contact our admission office if you have any questions or require any assistance with the admission process. We look forward to welcoming you to the MarkJunior School community.
