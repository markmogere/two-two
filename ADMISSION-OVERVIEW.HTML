ADMISSION OVERVIEW

MarkJunior School is a leading institution that provides quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our school is known for its excellent academic programs, co-curricular activities, and a nurturing learning environment. We welcome students who are eager to learn, grow and make a positive impact on society.
Our admission process is designed to be transparent, fair, and objective. We evaluate each applicant's academic and extracurricular achievements, personal qualities, and potential to contribute positively to our school community.
The admission process for MarkJunior School starts with an online application. The requires basic personal and academic information, and it should be submitted along with supporting documents such as transcripts, recommendation letters, and a recent passport-sized photograph.
After receiving the applications, our admission committee reviews them and invites eligible candidates for an interview. The interview is conducted by our experienced faculty members, and it helps us understand the student's personality, interests, and goals.
Based on the application and interview, the admission committee evaluates the candidates and selects those who meet our admission criteria. The selected candidates are then offered admission to MarkJunior School.
We understand that choosing the right school for your child is a critical decision, and we encourage parents to visit our campus, attend our information sessions, and interact with our faculty and staff to learn more about our school's philosophy and programs.
At MarkJunior School, we value diversity and inclusivity, and we welcome students from all backgrounds to join our community. We strive to provide equal opportunities to all our students and ensure that they receive the best possible education to help them succeed in their academic and personal lives
