SPORTS

MarkJunior School recognizes the importance of sports in a student's physical and mental development. We believe that participating in sports not only promotes physical fitness but also instills valuable life skills such as teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. With that in mind, we offer a wide range of sports activities to our students. Our sports program at MarkJunior School is comprehensive and caters to the interests and abilities of all our students. We offer sports such as basketball, football, volleyball, athletics, and swimming. Our students have access to state-of-the-art sports facilities that include a basketball court, football field, volleyball court, athletics track, and a swimming pool. We have a team of experienced coaches who work with our students to develop their skills and abilities. Our coaches not only focus on the technical aspects of the sport but also help our students develop important life skills such as leadership, communication, and teamwork. At MarkJunior School, we encourage our students to participate in inter-school competitions and tournaments. Our sports teams have participated in various competitions and have brought home several accolades. We believe that participating in these competitions not only helps our students develop their skills but also instills a sense of pride and achievement. We understand that not all our students are interested in competitive sports. Therefore, we also offer recreational sports activities that allow our students to have fun while staying physically active. These activities include yoga, aerobics, and dance classes. At MarkJunior School, we believe that sports play a crucial role in a student's overall development. Therefore, we encourage our students to participate in sports and make the most of the opportunities provided. We believe that our sports program helps our students develop the skills and confidence they need to succeed in all areas of their lives
