Who we are

Overview
We are a vibrant and dynamic community of learners dedicated to providing our students with a top-quality education that prepares them for success in a rapidly changing world. Our school has a Rich Heritage that dates back to its founding in 1965.
Over the years, we have remained true to our mission of providing an exceptional education that combines academic excellence, personal development, and community engagement. At MarkJunior School, we believe that education is not just about learning, but also about personal growth and character development. Our experienced and dedicated faculty work tirelessly to provide a challenging and stimulating academic program that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.
But we also know that education extends beyond the classroom. That's why we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities and service opportunities that allow our students to explore their interests, develop their talents, and contribute to their communities. Our rich heritage is an integral part of who we are at MarkJunior School.
We are proud of our history and traditions, and we strive to honor them by providing a world-class education that prepares our students for success in the 21st century. We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive community that celebrates diversity, equity, and inclusion. We believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness, and we work tirelessly to help our students reach their full potential.
Mission and Vision
Our mission and vision is to provide a comprehensive and high-quality education that is rooted in our core values of respect, integrity, innovation, and excellence. We strive to empower our students to become well-rounded, responsible, and successful members of society, and to instill in them a love for lifelong learning.
Academic Program
At MarkJunior School, we offer a challenging and diverse academic program that is designed to help students achieve their full potential. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to provide a balance between academic excellence, character development, and practical life skills. Our students are exposed to a wide range of subjects, including Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Social Sciences, and Humanities, and are encouraged to pursue their interests and passions through a variety of elective courses.
Faculty and Staff
Our staff and faculty are dedicated, experienced, and passionate about teaching and learning. They are committed to providing our students with the best possible educational experience and strive to create a supportive and nurturing learning environment. Our teachers are experts in their fields, with many holding advanced degrees and specialized training.
Student Life
MarkJunior School provides an engaging and vibrant student life that is designed to help students develop their interests, passions, and skills outside of the classroom. We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including athletics, music, drama, art, robotics, and community service. Our students are encouraged to get involved and to explore new experiences that will help them grow and develop as individuals.
Community Engagement
At MarkJunior School, we believe in the power of community engagement and the importance of giving back. We have a strong commitment to community service and encourage our students to participate in various community outreach programs. We also foster strong partnerships with local businesses, organizations, and institutions to provide our students with real-world experiences and opportunities.
History and Tradition
MarkJunior School has a proud history and tradition of providing an outstanding education to students for over 50 years. Our school was founded in 1965 and has since become a leader in educational excellence, innovation, and community engagement. We take pride in our legacy and strive to honor it by continuing to provide an exceptional education to our students.
Diversity and Inclusion
MarkJunior School is committed to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion. We believe that a diverse and inclusive community is essential for providing an exceptional educational experience for our students. We welcome students from all backgrounds, cultures, and identities and strive to create a safe and welcoming environment for all.
Accreditation and Recognition
MarkJunior School is accredited by the National Association of Independent Schools (NAIS) and is a member of the Independent Schools Association of the Central States (ISACS). We are also recognized as a National Blue Ribbon School by the U.S. Department of Education, an honor that recognizes our commitment to academic excellence and innovation.
